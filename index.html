Introduction

Introducing MultiNet: a comprehensive benchmark for evaluating Vision-Language and Vision-Language-Action models on their generalist capabilities. With Multinet, we aim to provide:

  • The largest collection of open-source datasets for evaluating Vision-Language and Vision-Language-Action models
  • Open-source software infrastructure for downloading, managing, and utilizing the benchmark data
  • Open-source code to translate RL and Robotics datasets into a unified Tensorflow Datasets format
  • A general framework for mapping VLMs to other modality classes, with particular emphasis on action spaces

In this initial release, we present the results of evaluating SOTA VLM, VLA and generalist model on 20 OpenX datasets. We aim to quickly iterate and expand our evaluation results to more datasets within Multinet.
