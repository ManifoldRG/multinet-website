Introducing Multinet

We present Multinet, a comprehensive benchmark for evaluating generalist models across vision, language, and action. It consolidates diverse datasets and standardizes protocols to assess multimodal understanding and action generation capabilities of SoTA VLMs and VLAs. Multinet provides extensive training data (800M+ image-text pairs, 1.3T language tokens, 35TB+ control data), varied evaluation tasks (e.g., captioning, VQA, robotics, game-playing, commonsense reasoning, simulated locomotion and manipulation, etc.), and an open-source toolkit to standardize the process of obtaining and utilizing robotics/RL data, aiming to highlight current model limitations and drive development of truly generalist AI.

GenESIS

Generalizable Extendable Stratified Inference System - A framework for mapping language models to actions

GenESIS

GenESIS structures prompts for action generation using:

  • System-level goals and constraints.
  • Task and environment context/rules.
  • Integration of visual input
  • Clear action space definitions.
  • Precise output formatting instructions.
